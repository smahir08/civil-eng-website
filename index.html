About BGSB University

Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah University has come into existence by an Act of the Jammu & Kashmir State Legislature called the Jammu & Kashmir Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah University Act No. XVI of 2002. The university is duly recognized by the University Grants Commission.

University Motto

The motto of the university is "Iqra Bism Rabbika Lazi Khalaq" which means "Read in the name of GOD who has created the Universe".

Location

The university is located at Rajouri in Jammu & Kashmir. Pir Panjal range in Rajouri, 154 KM from Jammu, the winter capital of Jammu and Kashmir State.

A major objective identified for the university in the Act is to impart quality education and undertake research in basic and job-oriented fields. Currently, the university offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Business Management, Financial Management, Computer Sciences, Information Technology, Arabic, Kashmiri, Mathematics and B.Tech in Electronics and Communication, Computer Science & Engineering and Information Technology.

About Rajouri

Rajouri is a town in Rajouri district in Jammu and Kashmir, India. Rajouri is about 155 km (96 mi) from Srinagar and 150 km from Jammu city on the Poonch Highway. Baba Ghulam Shah Badshah University is based in the town. Rajouri is 150 km from Jammu via Sunderbani-Nowshera and 130 km via Akhnoor (J&K). The climate of Rajouri is somewhat cooler than the neighboring plains. Summers are short and pleasant.
